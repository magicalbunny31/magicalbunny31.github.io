who was gentleberry16 gerek?

  • the persona behind gentleberry16 gerek was a cool dude who primarily made youtube videos based around Flood Escape 2
  • he had 50k+ subscribers on youtube and had been creating content since 2016
  • here are some other cool facts about him, taken from an archive of his roblox account:
    • he loves to listen to: classical music, techno, ncs, jazz, and hip-hop
    • he used to play the piano (until it broke)

      • this is why we love you, gentle!

why is he no longer here?

  • he has made a statement of his departure from the internet: below will be a brief summary of it (however, you should read his statement to truly understand his full story!)
    • it was intentional - something he had been planning to do for a while now
    • his content "hadn't been getting the attention it used to years ago" (and his discord server had been pretty much dead for a long time then)
    • a large portion of his enjoyment and passion for creating video had cooled off
    • not knowing whether a video was "good enough" was taking a toll on him for the worse
    • at the time, flood escape 2 was losing popularity
    • as he took breaks, he enjoyed those periods of time being away from the internet and saw "how precious and enjoyable life truly is when your eyes aren't constantly glued to a computer screen"
    • looking back at the things he did on the internet, such as his youtube channel, made him cringe a lot; and, as a result, he removed his footprint online (at least related to the alias "gentleberry16 gerek")
